Curated Experiences

Beyond the Room

From misty forest trails to crackling bonfires — every day at ZephyrStays is a new story waiting to be written.

🌿

Nature Trails

Guided walks through ancient spice and coffee plantations with our resident naturalist. Discover flora you won't find in any textbook.

🦅

Bird Watching

Coorg is home to 300+ bird species. Dawn excursions with binoculars and field guides — a treat for ornithology enthusiasts.

💧

Waterfall Trek

A 3km trail through dense jungle leads to a pristine hidden waterfall. Perfect for thrill-seekers and photographers alike.

🔥

Bonfire Nights

Gather under a canopy of stars around a crackling bonfire. Roasted marshmallows, folk music, and the scent of the forest — pure magic.

🧘

Yoga Sessions

Morning yoga on our open-air deck overlooking the valley — start your day with clarity, breathwork, and the sound of birds awakening.

🍛

Local Cuisine Tour

Visit a Kodava home kitchen and learn the secrets of Coorg's legendary pork curry and Kadambuttu — a true cultural immersion.
